    View the list of webstores that are configured in the WooCommerce Connector

    On the Webstores page, you can view the list of webstores that are configured in the WooCommerce Connector.

    The Webstores page is accessible from the Business Manager and Sales Order Processor role centers.

    To view the list of webstores that are configured in the WooCommerce Connector

    On the role center menu, choose WooCommerce > Webstores.

    The Webstores page opens.
